Meet
the makers

Sky Forge is more than a platform, it's a pilot-first community.

We started by delivering high-quality resources like flight training guides and educational infographics.

Today, we're expanding to solve one of aviation's biggest challenges: access to mental and physical health support without fear of professional repercussions.

Our secure, anonymous application acts as a trusted bridge: pilots submit forms privately, we ensure full anonymity, and licensed Aviation Medical Examiners provide expert guidance and resources all returned through Sky Forge with zero direct contact.
