Sky Forge

Confidential pilot support

Sky Forge gives pilots a safer way to raise wellbeing concerns early and receive support guidance or referral information — without involving your employer in individual reports.

Sky Forge is a confidential wellbeing navigation and referral platform. It does not diagnose, treat, prescribe, respond to emergencies or make aviation licensing decisions.

Step 1 of 4

Partner access

Access to this pilot programme is provided through our launch partner. Enter the one-time access code you were given. The code only confirms eligibility — it is kept separate from anything you submit and is never attached to your report.

Step 2 of 4

Before you continue

Sky Forge is not an emergency service. If you are in immediate danger, at risk of harming yourself or someone else, or require urgent medical assistance, please contact local emergency services or go to the nearest emergency department.

Sky Forge does not diagnose, treat, prescribe or make aviation licensing decisions. This platform helps you submit a confidential wellbeing concern and receive support guidance or referral information.

Sky Forge does

  • Provide a confidential, privacy-first intake pathway
  • Route your concern to a clinical review pathway
  • Return support guidance, resources or referral information
  • Protect identifying details wherever possible

Sky Forge does not

  • Diagnose, treat or prescribe
  • Replace emergency services or urgent medical care
  • Determine fitness to fly or make licensing decisions
  • Share individual reports with your employer
